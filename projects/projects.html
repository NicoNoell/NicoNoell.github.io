A*-Pathfinding

If you don't know the A*-Pathfinding Algorithm (pronounced "A-Star"), it's an Algorithm to find the shortest path between two points. It has a multitude of applications, the most notible being of course car navigation systems or network and it works similar to Dijekstra's Algorithm↗.
Out of interest, I reprogrammed it myself in python and made a Youtube-Video↗ about it explaining the rough outlines of it.
